✴ about me

I am a rising third-year undergraduate at Georgia Institute of Technology working towards a Bachelor’s degree in Computational Media with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction and Interaction Design. This unique degree program allows me to develop a foundation in computing supplemented with design fundamentals that supports me in working alongside fellow impact-driven individuals and startups to tackle real-world problems within the social good space.

Beyond creative computing, I have a long-running passion for accessible and liberatory STEM education for underrepresented youth. By arming these student with high-level technical skills, they become the creators of impactful solutions that their community needs most.

When I’m not working, you can find me reading about political history, making niche spotify playlists, or biking around Atlanta.
